The Universe


The universe is composed of dark energy, dark matter, and ordinary matter. It has been expanding since the Big Bang 13.8 billion years ago. The observable universe is about 93 billion light-years in diameter.


Quick Facts = >


  • Age of Universe: 13.8B yrs

  • Observable size: 93B light-years

  • Speed of light: 300,000 km/s

Galaxy

A galaxy is a system of stars, dust, and dark matter bound by gravity. They range in size from dwarfs with a few thousand stars to supergiants with trillions.

Milky Way

The Milky Way is a barred spiral galaxy containing our Solar System. It appears as a hazy band in the night sky made up of distant stars.
